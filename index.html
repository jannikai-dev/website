FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Is this just ChatGPT?
No. ChatGPT can't text your customers. Jannik AI is a complete system — it connects to WhatsApp and SMS, knows your business inside out, and handles real conversations with real customers on your behalf.
What about my data?
Your data is private, encrypted, and secured over VPN. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for anything other than running your assistant. For maximum security, ask about our on-premise option — data never leaves your building.
Do I need to learn new software?
No. You text — that's it. Your admin channel works through the same messaging apps you already use. No dashboards, no portals, no training required.
What if the AI says something wrong?
We configure your AI with conservative settings. Big decisions get routed to you for approval. We monitor weekly and fine-tune continuously. The AI is trained on your specific business — it doesn't make things up.

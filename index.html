What It Honestly Can't Do

We'd rather under-promise and over-deliver.

We're not going to pretend AI can do everything. Here's where it stops — and where you still need a human.

  • ✗
    No human-sounding phone calls. AI-generated voice calls aren't there yet. We handle text and email — the channels where AI actually works well today.
  • ✗
    No complex judgment calls. A tenant threatening legal action? A customer disputing a major bill? That's you. AI flags it and escalates — it doesn't play lawyer or negotiator.
  • ✗
    No physical tasks. It can text your plumber to go fix the leak. It can't fix the leak.
  • ✗
    It's not 100% — it's 70–80%. It handles the repetitive, predictable, high-volume work. The other 20% — the stuff that actually requires human judgment — that's still yours. And honestly, that's the part you're good at.
